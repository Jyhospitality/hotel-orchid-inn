Hotel Orchid Inn

Welcome to Hotel Orchid Inn near the bustling and convenient Sakinaka Metro Station. Our newly built hotel offers 20 air-conditioned rooms for your comfort and relaxation.

Whether you're in town for business or pleasure, our hotel's prime location near the metro station makes it easy for you to get around and explore all that Mumbai has to offer. We are dedicated to making your stay with us memorable, so book your room at Hotel Orchid Inn today! At Hotel Orchid Inn, we provide comfortable and convenient accommodations for all of our guests. Our 20 air-conditioned rooms are equipped with all the amenities you need for a relaxing stay.

Stay connected with free high-speed Wi-Fi available in all rooms. Unwind in front of a flat-screen TV and stay cool with the air-conditioning system. Enjoy hot and cold running water for a refreshing shower. We understand that parking can be a concern, so we offer secure and convenient parking for all our guests. Our 24-hour reception ensures that someone is always available to assist you, no matter the time of day.
